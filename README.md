## 📂 Projects

### 🏥 Medicare Spending Per Beneficiary — Outlier & Readmission Analysis
**Tools:** Excel · Power BI | **Domain:** Healthcare Analytics

> How can we pinpoint hospitals with unusually high Medicare spending 
and link those costs to quality and readmission patterns — so finance 
and quality teams can target interventions?

- Analyzed 2,004 U.S. hospitals using CMS Provider Data
- Identified 36 High Outlier hospitals averaging 0.188 above peer 
group median
- Built a two-page Power BI dashboard for CFO and Quality stakeholder 
audiences
- Found inverse relationship between hospital rating and MSPB cost 
(Low band = 1.02 vs High band = 0.98)
- Discovered FL, CA, PA account for nearly half of all High Outliers

👉 [View Project](https://github.com/Adri-Pineda/Medicare_Spending_Per_Beneficiary_PowerBI)
